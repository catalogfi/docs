# Catalog Overview

## What is Catalog?

[Catalog](https://catalog.fi/) is a bridge-less cross-chain protocol. It is designed to provide a seamless way for your Bitcoin, or any other asset, to experience DeFi, GameFi, and SocialFi.

- **Catalog Accounts** – Catalog Accounts support the way you interact with Web3. These are non-custodial accounts that allow you to store assets from any blockchain, and use them without waiting for confirmation delays.

- **Catalog Swaps** – Catalog Swaps offer you a way to execute cross-chain swaps instantly and atomically, without the need for gas tokens.

- **Catalog Wallet** – Catalog Wallet is a browser extension that enables seamless support for cross-chain assets on all dApps, meaning you can transact Bitcoin and other assets directly on your favorite exchange or NFT marketplace.
